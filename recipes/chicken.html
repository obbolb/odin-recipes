Khoa Man Gai

Indulge in the irresistible allure of Thai cuisine with our Classic Khao Man Gai recipe.

This dish is a delightful symphony of flavors, featuring tender poached chicken atop fragrant jasmine rice.

Every bite is a burst of aromatic goodness that will transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Thailand.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 3-4 pounds)
  • 4 slices of ginger
  • 4 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 2 stalks of lemongrass, cut into 2-inch pieces and smashed
  • Salt, to taste
  • Water for boiling
  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth (from boiling the chicken)
  • 4-6 slices of ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 pandan leaves (optional, for aroma)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh cucumber slices
  • Fresh cilantro sprigs

Recipe Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken:
    • In a large pot, bring enough water to cover the chicken to a boil.
    • Add the chicken, ginger slices, crushed garlic, smashed lemongrass, and a generous pinch of salt to the pot.
    • Reduce the heat to a gentle simmer and poach the chicken for about 45-60 minutes, or until fully cooked. You can check doneness by inserting a knife into the thigh; the juices should run clear when done.
    • Once cooked, remove the chicken from the pot and let it cool. Save the chicken broth for cooking the rice.
  2. Prepare the Rice:
    • Wash the jasmine rice thoroughly under cold water until the water runs clear.
    • In a large saucepan, heat a little oil and add minced garlic and ginger slices. Saut√© until fragrant.
    • Add the washed rice to the saucepan and stir to coat the rice with the aromatics.
    • Pour in the chicken broth (use the broth from boiling the chicken), add pandan leaves (if using), and season with salt. Bring to a boil.
    • Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
    • Remove from heat and let it sit, covered, for an additional 10 minutes. Fluff the rice with a fork.
  3. Assemble Khao Man Gai:
    • Once the chicken has cooled, rub the skin with a little salt. Then, chop the chicken into serving-sized pieces.
    • To serve, place a portion of the fragrant jasmine rice on a plate, top with slices of poached chicken, and garnish with cucumber slices and cilantro sprigs.
